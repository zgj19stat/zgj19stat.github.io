I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student from [School of Mathematical Sciences](https://math.ucas.edu.cn/), [University of Chinese Academic of Science](https://www.ucas.ac.cn/), advised by [Professor Sanguo Zhang](https://people.ucas.edu.cn/~sgzhang?language=en). Recent works focus on Transfer Learning, Multi-Layers/Time-varying Networks Analysis. 

I will visit the [Department of Statistics and Data Science](https://www.stat.nus.edu.sg/), [National University of Singapore](https://www.nus.edu.sg/), as a visiting Ph.D. student from September 1, 2026 to August 31, 2027, hosted by [Assistant Professor Doudou Zhou](https://doudouzhou.github.io/).

I am actively seeking and welcoming research collaborations. My interests cover many of the current focal points in the machine learning and network analysis community, with a particular emphasis on:

- Domain Generalization, Multi-source Data Analysis

- Latent Space Model, Hub/Community Detection

- ...

I am passionate about exploring these areas from both theoretical and applied perspectives. If you share these interests or have an idea for a potential project, please feel free to contact me via email to begin a discussion.
